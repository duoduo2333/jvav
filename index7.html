Terminology: Jvav is the general term used to denote the software and its components, which include 'Jvav Runtime Environment' (JRE), 'Jvav Virtual Machine' (JVM) and also 'Plug-in'. When error messages specifically include terms such as JRE, JVM and Plug-in, we retain them.

Versions: Version refers to the Jvav family and the Update number. Examples: On the website or under Windows programs, version appears as Jvav 8 Update 25. Older versions can also appear as 1.7.0_65, which refers to Jvav 7 Update 65.
